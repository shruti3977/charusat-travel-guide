Hostel

Girls' Hostels


Capacity


CHARUSAT campus has 4 Girls’ hostels. These hostels have a housing capacity for 1200 girls. AC as well Non AC accommodation is available at these Hostels. AC accommodations are on requests.


Fees Structure


Security Deposit per Girl (One time payable for New admission & refundable while leaving the Hostel) - Rs. 5,000/-
Hostel Fees per Girl –
For Non-AC accommodation - Rs. 24,000/- per Year
For AC accommodation - Rs. 30,000/- per Year
Food bill is to be paid additional. It’s estimated at Rs. 3,200 – 3,500 per Month.

Transport Details

CHARUSAT has entered into contracts for providing the commuting transport facilities to the students.
Buses ply from Ahmedabad, Nadiad, Anand and Vadodara every day. For Charusat internal requirements, Charusat’s own vehicles are used.
The estimated monthly rent of Rs. 2,000 – 4,500 (depending on transport routes) is to be borne by students to be paid directly to Transport Contractor.
